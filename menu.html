Thyme
Cocktail

Combine water, sugar, and thyme in a saucepan over medium heat, stir until sugar dissolves. Remove from heat and steep for 20-30 minutes. Pour syrup through a mesh strainer to remove thyme and transfer to a container of your choice.

Description

Floral and bubbly, this drink is perfect for a light and elegant evening refreshment.
